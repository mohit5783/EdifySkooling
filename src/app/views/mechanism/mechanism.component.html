Edify Skooling - Overview

Edify Skooling is one of the world's most comprehensive ERP solution ever designed for managing schools and any educational institution. It has become a leader in the bandwagon of School Management System integrated with the mobile application. Edify Skooling is a powerful online community that brings an educational institution's management, parents, teachers, and students on one common interactive platform. We provides a cloud-based solution with complete automated experience with all backend administration and data management activities of the institution. We support over numerous schools and colleges in multiple countries with 73 languages.

The application's flexibility and ease of access enables your institution to choose, customize and deploy the modules based on your unique requirements. Its fully integrated and advanced set of modules enhance teaching, learning, information-sharing, administration, and data management. Hence, Edify Skooling augments the institution's brand image and reputation with increased efficiency, productivity and profitability.

Administration

Administration and management of schools, colleges, universities and educational institute is a very tedious task. Edify Skooling helps in making this simple and easy.
